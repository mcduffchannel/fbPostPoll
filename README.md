# fbPostPoll - Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 27 December 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how this application (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects information obtained through Facebook (Meta) APIs.

The App is intended for personal, testing, or internal use and does not operate as a commercial service.

---

## Information We Collect

The App may access the following information via the Facebook Graph API, subject to permissions granted by the user:

* Public profile information
* Page or post metadata (such as post IDs and timestamps)
* Engagement-related data where explicitly authorised

The App **does not collect**:

* Passwords
* Private messages
* Financial information
* Sensitive personal data

---

## How Information Is Used

Information accessed by the App is used solely to:

* Retrieve metadata such as post creation timestamps
* Display or process data requested by the user
* Perform debugging, testing, or analysis

No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

---

## Data Storage and Retention

The App does not permanently store personal data.
Any data retrieved via the Facebook API is processed transiently and discarded unless explicitly required for the App’s functionality.

---

## Data Sharing

The App does not share data with third parties.
All access to Facebook data complies with Meta’s Platform Terms and applicable policies.

---

## User Control

Users can revoke the App’s access at any time via their Facebook account settings.

---

## Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect data accessed by the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
